SECTION 1: VEHICLE ONBOARDING — OVERVIEW FLOW

Every vehicle entering the Ola Cars fleet must complete all 9 onboarding stages before it can be set to ACTIVE and made available for rental. No stage can be skipped. The system enforces sequential completion — the next stage unlocks only when the previous stage is fully complete and approved.



STEP

1

Procurement & Purchase Entry

Owner: Operations Staff + Finance Staff

System: New vehicle record created; PO and purchase price recorded; accounting GL entry initiated

Action: Enter supplier, purchase price, payment method, branch assignment



STEP

2

Vehicle Basic Details & Registration

Owner: Operations Staff

System: VIN validated for duplicates; vehicle profile populated; odometer baseline set for maintenance alerts

Action: Enter make, model, year, VIN, engine number, fuel type, colour, GPS serial



STEP

3

Registration & Legal Documents Upload

Owner: Operations Staff

System: Expiry dates stored; alert schedule set; registration number indexed for search

Action: Upload registration certificate, road tax disc, roadworthiness certificate, number plate photos



STEP

4

Insurance Policy Setup

Owner: Finance Staff + Branch Manager

System: Insurance expense schedule created in accounting; auto-renewal alerts configured

Action: Upload policy document; enter provider, policy number, coverage, premium, expiry date



STEP

5

Importation & Landed Cost (Imported Vehicles Only)

Owner: Operations Staff + Finance Staff

System: Landed cost calculated automatically; capitalised as fixed asset value in accounting GL

Action: Enter shipping, customs duty, port charges; upload customs clearance documents



STEP

6

Pre-Entry Physical Inspection

Owner: Operations Staff

System: Inspection results stored against vehicle record; failed items auto-generate maintenance work orders; mandatory photos uploaded

Action: Complete 23-point inspection checklist; rate each item; upload minimum 6 exterior photos + odometer photo



STEP

7

Accounting Setup & Depreciation

Owner: Finance Staff

System: GL entries auto-posted on save; depreciation schedule activated from purchase/arrival date; asset registers updated

Action: Confirm asset posting; select depreciation method (straight-line or reducing balance); set useful life



STEP

8

GPS Device Activation & Configuration

Owner: Operations Staff

System: Vehicle linked to GPS API; live location visible on map; geofence and alert thresholds applied; driver behaviour monitoring active

Action: Confirm GPS serial installed; run sync test; assign geofence zone; configure speed and idle alerts



STEP

9

Branch Manager Final Approval & Activation

Owner: Branch Manager

System: Vehicle status set to AVAILABLE; appears on fleet dashboard; rental booking enabled; maintenance schedule active

Action: Review onboarding summary report; confirm all stages complete; click Activate Vehicle

5.2  Depreciation Methods Supported

Method

How It Works

When to Use

Straight-Line

Equal depreciation charge each year: (Cost - Residual Value) / Useful Life

Standard fleet vehicles with predictable usage; simplest method; default setting

Reducing Balance

Higher depreciation in early years: NBV x Depreciation Rate %

High-use or luxury vehicles that lose value faster in early years

Useful Life (typical)

Cars: 5–8 years  |  SUVs / Pickups: 6–10 years  |  Commercial: 8–12 years

Finance Staff sets per vehicle; Country Manager approves if > standard range

Residual Value

Estimated disposal value at end of useful life; default 10–15% of purchase price

Finance Staff sets on activation; Country Manager approves changes
